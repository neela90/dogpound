Why Invest in Dog Pound ?

Dog Pound token holders can benefit not only through static rewards, but also through the contract buy-back process. In this process, the contract is responsible for buying back some tokens and burning them on each sell. In majority of the cases, you will not see 2 or more sell transactions at any time!
